About

Hello, I am Eloïs Henry and I am a first-year student in BUT MMI in Montbéliard.

This website is a project we have to complete on the topic "prehistoric future." Its purpose is to present our vision of the subject.

I chose to showcase sneakers by tracing their evolution while imagining a futuristic approach that reflects innovation and the impact of technology on their design.
