Glossary

Streetwear: Streetwear is a casual style of clothing that blends elements of urban culture, sportswear, and high-end fashion. Born from skate, surf, and hip-hop scenes, it's characterized by loose-fitting clothes, graphic tees, hoodies, sneakers, and bold logos.

Reseller: Reselling is the activity of buying products, often limited edition items like sneakers, to resell at a higher price. Resellers take advantage of high demand and scarcity of items to generate profit, thus creating a secondary market.

Hoverboard: In Back to the Future 2, the hoverboard is a futuristic board that floats above the ground, allowing its user to move without touching the ground. Introduced in an iconic scene where Marty McFly travels to 2015, the hoverboard functions like a skateboard without wheels, gliding on air.

Avant-garde: Avant-garde style is an artistic approach that seeks to innovate by pushing the boundaries of established conventions. In fashion, it manifests through bold and futuristic designs, using unconventional shapes, materials, and ideas, often perceived as ahead of their time.

Psychedelic decor: Psychedelic decor is characterized by vivid colors, fluid shapes, and surrealistic patterns, creating an immersive atmosphere and distorting the perception of reality.
